About Radiant Roots School

Built on Passion, Driven by Purpose

Founded by Archana Zulfiqar, an accomplished educator with years of experience in early childhood education, Radiant Roots School was born out of a deep commitment to making learning joyful and meaningful. Archana's vision has always been clear: to create a school where each child receives attention, encouragement, and the right guidance during their formative years.

At Radiant Roots School, our approach is child-centric. We believe that education is not one-size-fits-all. Our methods are rooted in modern pedagogical theories, fused with practical, real-world experiences that resonate with young learners.

Our staff includes trained professionals passionate about early education, ensuring your child is always in good hands. Whether it’s storytelling, sensory play, early numeracy, or language development, every activity is carefully designed to make learning effective and enjoyable.
